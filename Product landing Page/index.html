A notification alert on a smartwatch keeps you informed in real-time. Vibrations or visual cues notify you of calls, messages, and app updates, ensuring you stay connected and updated at a glance.

Live chat on a smartwatch enables real-time communication on your wrist. Stay connected with friends or colleagues through instant messaging, enhancing convenience and ensuring quick responses wherever you are.

The heart rate tracker on a smartwatch continuously monitors your heart's rhythm, providing real-time data on your cardiovascular health. It aids in fitness tracking and promotes overall well-being.
